Conformal Jugglers

Here you see three jugglers passing clubs in various juggling patterns. The video is shot by Henry Segerman with a spherical camera capturing an entire viewing globe.

Move the white points to change the geometry of the setup. (Mathematically speaking you see conformal transformations of repeated versions of the original video).

Software: CindyJS
Deformation made possible by connecting CindyJS to WebGL and calculating transformations on the GPU.
